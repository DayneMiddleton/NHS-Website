Welcome to Upper-Gastrointestinal Support

This is a website that has been created by the UHNM, to give you helpful advice and useful information on how to stay fit and healthy before and after UGI surgery.

There is an Application that can be useful to help you in the prehabilitation process

Having surgery can put a lot of significant amount of stress, and often will leave you feeling weak and tired, to improve your daily lifestyle routine

Don't worry, there is an application that can help you to improve yourself, by giving you helpful useful information and diagrams to revamp on your healthy living lifestyle that can get you into the mood to be fit and healthy after surgery.

More information about the application can be found in the prehabilitation webpage
